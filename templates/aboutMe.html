The Blessed Wardrobe

was launched in 2018 by Urvi Dama for women of all sizes and ages. TBW offers an array of Indo western ethnic wear. Loved for cowls and drapes.
Founder and designer Urvi Dama, caught the entrepreneurial bug at a young age. Her clothing line debut was made through an exhibition in Mumbai during her college days at the age of 21, the exhibition was an absolute hit and all products were sold out on the first day itself. After that, there was no stopping for this young girl and her big dreams.

Journey so far

TBW has done over 80+ exhibtions in Mumbai like The Lil Flea, Style cracker, The Wedding Junction show, Marriage Mantra etc.
After receiving love and appreciation from the women of Bombay, it was now time to expand TBW’s magic Pan India. TBW started supplying 20 + Multi designer stores all over India and now in 2021, we are ready to explore the online world through this website.
Today Urvi along with her sister in law - Jasmeet Dama runs TBW with immense love and hardwork with the aim to dress all body types at a budget.
When asked what keeps them going on hard days and difficult times like covid, “OMG! It’s so pretty, You really have that in my size?” Is what keeps them going!
